Murdoch in Motion: A Selfie Within the Pentatonic Glow | Unbrushed Hair | Pentatonic Color System Installation | Kumu Art Museum

Murdoch takes a selfie inside the dimly lit exhibition space, standing in front of the Raoul Kurvitz: Pentatonic Color System installation. The glow of 20 monitors flickers across his face, casting an eerie mix of colors—blues, reds, and muted yellows—reflected in his glasses. His expression is both contemplative and intrigued as he captures the abstract visual sequences on the screens behind him. The ambient soundscape by Ariel Lagle hums softly in the background, adding an immersive layer to the experience. Murdoch's shadow stretches slightly on the floor, a subtle reminder of his presence within the digital and programmed art environment.
